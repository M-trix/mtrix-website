About Club

M'Trix is the programming club of SGGSIE&T, where we group of people are passionate about programing, tech and this is the only place where being geeky nerd won't irritate others!
We, in M'trix, will gather 'passionate' programers, people who really live for geeky stuffs, and some of the most excellent programers in the institute will guide you through the world of code, realm of technology, dark universe of ethical hacking and oceans of 1's and 0's.

Cyber Security

Cyber security refers to the body of technologies, processes, and practices designed to protect networks, devices, programs, and data from attack, damage, or unauthorized access.

Data structure & algo

Algorithm is a step-by-step procedure, which defines a set of instructions to be executed in a certain order to get the desired output.While data structure is a collection of data values, the relationships among them, and the functions or operations that can be applied to the data.

Game Development

Game Development is the art of creating games and describes the design, development and release of a game. It may involve concept generation, design, build, test and release.

Animation

Animation is a way of making a movie from many still images. The images are put together one after another, and then played at a fast speed to give the illusion of movement.
